About

Madhusthali took birth amidst nature, and grew up to spread its own branches to offer shade to the saplings who can grow and freely stretch their wings. Madhusthali Vidyapeeth was founded in 1997 by Madhusthali Vidyapeeth Trust administered by the trustees of the reputed and renowned MCKV Group running educational institutions like M. C. Kejriwal Vidyapeeth and MCKV Institute of Engineering, Kolkata. The school is a co-ed residential school affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination, New Delhi.
